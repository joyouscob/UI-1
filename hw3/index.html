Gesture-Operated Video Player Help

To control the video player, simply use the left mouse button to draw the corresponding gesture anywhere on the screen (excluding the footer). When you've drawn the gesture, in as many strokes as you'd like, right-click. That's it.

To bring up this help window, use the gesture below, or hit the H key.

Play/Pause:
Increase Volume:
Decrease Volume:
Mute On/Off:
Seek Forward:
Seek Reverse:
Increase Playback Speed:
Decrease Playback Speed:
